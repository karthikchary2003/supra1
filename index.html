Toyota Supra

Engine

The Supra has a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine that's closed-deck for strength, and includes connecting rods, a crankshaft, and pistons for enhanced power.

Interior

The Supra has an 8.8-inch multimedia system for easy access to audio, communication, and navigation functions.

Safety

The Supra has standard and available technologies to help you see better, park better, avoid collisions, and mitigate damage.

Braking systems

The Supra has an optional Sport Pack that includes a sports braking system with red aluminum Brembo calipers.

Pricing

Toyota Supra On Road Price in New Delhi starts Rs. 85 Lakh.
